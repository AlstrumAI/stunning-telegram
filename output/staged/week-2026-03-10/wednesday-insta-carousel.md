## Slide 2
**WHAT ARE DARK POOLS?**
Private exchanges run by Goldman, JP Morgan, Morgan Stanley.
Orders matched without being visible to the public book.
~60 dark pools operating in the US.
You can't see the orders until after execution.

## Slide 3
**WHY THEY EXIST**
A pension fund selling 5M shares of MSFT doesn't put it on NASDAQ.
Every algo would front-run it.
Instead: matched internally. Price doesn't move. Trade prints after.
By the time you see it on TradingView, it's already done.
