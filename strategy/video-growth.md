# Video Growth Strategy

## What This File Is
Instructions for CC when generating video data JSONs from the current edition. Video reels are the highest-reach format at low follower count — Instagram pushes them to non-followers. Every video is an acquisition tool: hook a new viewer, make them feel smarter, make them follow.

## How Video Fits the Weekly Workflow
Sunday batch generation: CC reads `published/current-edition.md`, selects the strongest video hooks from that edition, generates JSONs following the 5-beat structure below. Render on the Windows box. Post Wed and Fri (or whenever fits the deploy checklist).

Video is NOT "the newsletter in video form." Video is the HOOK that pulls new people toward the newsletter.

## Selecting What Becomes a Video (From the Current Edition)
Pick 2-3 hooks. Score each on:
- **Pattern interrupt potential** (1-5): Would this make someone stop scrolling in 1.5 seconds?
- **Simplicity** (1-5): Can it be explained in one concept, 15-25 seconds?
- **Share trigger** (1-5): Would someone send this to a friend to look smart?
- **Visual potential** (1-5): Numbers counting up, before/after, comparison?

Prioritize: surprising data points, concepts that contradict retail beliefs, simple mechanical explanations.
Skip: multi-step analysis, plays/watchlist updates, build-in-public.

## The 5-Beat Structure (Every Video, Non-Negotiable)

### Beat 1: Pattern Interrupt (0-2 seconds)
A single provocative number or statement. Must work with SOUND OFF (text-first, highlighted keyword). The viewer decides in 1.5 seconds whether to keep watching.
- GOOD: "465,230 contracts. Then zero." / "58% liquidated in five days."
- BAD: "Let's talk about Treasury positioning." / "This week in markets..."

### Beat 2: Curiosity Gap (2-5 seconds)
Create tension only resolved by watching. Two patterns:
- "Most people think [X]. The data says [Y]."
- "Everyone saw [price move]. Nobody saw [the positioning that predicted it]."
If they make it past 3 seconds, they'll likely finish. This beat wins or loses watch time.

### Beat 3: The Reveal (5-15 seconds)
The actual data or insight. ONE concept. Not a summary. One thing.
- Visual emphasis on the key number (animated counter, highlight, color change)
- One new data point every 3-4 seconds max
- Let the data build tension through the sequence

### Beat 4: The Reframe (15-20 seconds)
The "That's not X. That's Y." moment. This is the SHARE TRIGGER.
Must feel like an insight the viewer can repeat: "Did you know 35% of all trading is invisible?"
- "That's not a pullback. That's a regime shift."
- "That's not manipulation. That's architecture."
- "That's not noise. That's the market telling you where the next move lives."

### Beat 5: CTA (last 2-3 seconds)
Not "link in bio." Not "subscribe." Make following feel like gaining an edge:
- "This data is free. I break it down every Monday. Follow @genghiskai."
- "The Relay updates every Friday at alstrum.ai. Follow to catch it."
- "I publish what institutions don't want you to see. Every Monday."

## Algorithm Optimization

**Watch time** (most important): Keep 15-30 seconds. Front-load hook. End before they expect it.
**Shares** (second): The reframe IS the share trigger. Design it quotable.
**Saves** (third): Educational content gets saved. Include one actionable thing.
**Profile visits → follows**: Every video must make the viewer think "this person has more I need to see."

## Series Branding
No episode numbers. Each composition has a series brand shown as overlay text:
- COTDataReveal → **THE NUMBERS**
- StructureEducation → **HOW THE MACHINE WORKS**
- RelayReel → **THE RELAY**
- WeeklyNumbers → **THE NUMBERS**
- ExitLiquidityHook → **EXIT LIQUIDITY**

The newsletter determines which videos get made each week. File naming uses the edition number (e.g., `cot-data-reveal-005.json`), but the video itself shows only the series name.

## Composition Selection Guide
Based on the hook type from the current edition:
- **Dramatic data trend** (numbers building week over week) → COTDataReveal
- **Retail vs institutional split** (someone got swept) → ExitLiquidityHook
- **How a mechanism works** (dark pools, collateral chains, sweeps) → StructureEducation
- **Cross-asset positioning update** (the Relay changed) → RelayReel
- **Rapid-fire biggest numbers this week** → WeeklyNumbers
